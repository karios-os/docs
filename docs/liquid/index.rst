Overview
========

Karios Cool is an integrated liquid cooling solution designed to address high power consumption and thermal management challenges in modern data center environments. This complete hardware and software solution prevents thermal throttling, maintains optimal performance, and ensures CPU utilization never exceeds safe operational limits while providing intelligent cooling control and energy efficiency optimization.

Karios Cool provides a comprehensive liquid cooling solution that combines advanced hardware and intelligent software to deliver superior thermal management for high-density data center deployments.
